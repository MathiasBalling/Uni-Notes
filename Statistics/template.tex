\section{Introduction}
Total probability \textbf{without} order is given by
$$\begin{pmatrix}
  n\\
  k
\end{pmatrix}=\frac{n!}{(n-k)!+k!}$$

Total probability \textbf{with} order is given by
$$\frac{n!}{(n-k)!}$$
